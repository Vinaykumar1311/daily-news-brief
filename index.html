ROW OVER KMC MOVE TO RENAME SUHRAWARDY AVENUE AFTER GOPAL MUKHERJEE

The Kolkata Municipal Corporation, currently run by a state-appointed administrator after the resignation of mayor Firhad Hakim, has courted controversy with its decision to rename Suhrawardy Avenue after Gopal Mukherjee, popularly known as Gopal Patha. The decision, taken during a Saturday meeting, has triggered a sharp political row in West Bengal. Proponents argue that the move honors a figure who protected communities during the historical communal violence of 1946. Opponents, however, criticize the renaming as a politically motivated attempt to polarize voters and rewrite local history. The municipal administration has defended the step as a routine renaming based on public demands.
